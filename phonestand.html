Phone stand

All I basically did was extrude a circle and then extruded 2 rectangles
(one in front and the other behind the thinly extruded circle) to act as "planks", one to support the circular base at an
angle to the ground, the other to support the phone against the circular base. It's a vey lazy design but due to time constraints
and my lack of knowledge on the software, it's the best I could come up with.



Extrude: Adding depth to a shape to create a basic object with a constant cross sectional area. Ex: circle -> extrudes to -> cylinder
